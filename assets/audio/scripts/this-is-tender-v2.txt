THIS IS TENDER — Welcome Audio Script (V2)
============================================
Duration: ~60 seconds
Voice: Warm, unhurried. WEARE voice — not salesy, not clinical.
Speaks to the user as someone who already matters.

---

[BEGIN SCRIPT]

Welcome to Tender.

This is your home — a living space that grows with you.

At the top, you'll find your portrait.
It's built from your assessments — a reflection of how you connect,
what you protect, and where you're growing.
It deepens every time you return.

Below that is your journey —
twelve steps of relational growth, personalized to your pattern.
Each one holds teachings, practices, and reflections designed for you.

Your daily rhythm lives here too —
a quick check-in, a journal prompt, a moment to notice
what's moving inside you.
Small moments, repeated, change everything.

And when you need someone to think with,
Nuance is here — your AI companion.
It knows your portrait and your journey.
It won't judge. It won't rush you.

Take your time.
Start with your first assessment — about ten minutes —
and everything else opens from there.

There's no wrong way to begin.
You're already here. That matters.

[END SCRIPT]

---

Notes:
- Paced for 60 seconds at natural speaking tempo (~130 wpm)
- Matches the 7-stop guided tour sequence: Welcome → Portrait → Journey → Daily Rhythm → Nuance → Explore → Ready
- Language rules honored: no "fix", no "score", no raw numbers
- Closing echoes the tour's final step ("You're Ready")
